Top Tourist Spots & Adventures

Sigay Aw-Asen

The tallest waterfall in Ilocos Region,
Aw-asen Falls, is located in Sto. Rosario, Sigay, Ilocos Sur.
The area of the falls is remote that keeps the place away from pollution,
the water comes from a spring uphill, and it is so fresh that you can even drink from it..

#TallestFalls In I.S #Sigay #History #Culture #IlocosSur

Dawara Falls

Discover the hidden gem in Barangay Poblacion,
where you can rejuvenate yourself by swimming in crystal-clear
waters while being captivated by a magnificent waterfall above.

#Figures #Suyo #Adventure #origin #History

Pinsal Falls

Located in Barangay Babal-lasioan, Santa Maria,
Ilocos Sur, the falls are around 85 feet high and known
for their cool, clear waters.
